George: She was absent today. I think she's sick.
Laluna: Really? I'm uncertain if she's sick. Yesterday she went to the market.
What does Laluna imply?
A. Laluna certain that the girl was sick.
B. George is lying.
C. George thinking that the girl was sick.
D. Laluna uncertain that the girl was sick.
E. All answers are right.

Answer: D (because it's expression of uncertainty).

Pergelangan tangan dan kaki termasuk sendi gerak (Diartrosis), yaitu Sendi Geser. a. sendi geser.

PEMBAHASAN

Sendi gerak atau disebut diartrosis adalah hubungan antar tulang yang cara kerjanya dapat digerakkan secara bebas.

-Macam macam sendi

Sendi engsel.

Sendi engsel yaitu sendi yang hanya digerakkan dengan satu arah, sendi engsel terbagi menjadi 3 yaitu sendi engsel siku, sendi engsel ruas-ruas, dan sendi engsel lutut.

Sendi peluru.

Sendu peluru yaitu sendi yang kemungkinan dapat digerakkan keseluruh arah secara bebas.

Sendi geser.

Sendi geser yaitu sendi yang kemungkinan sedikit gerakannya, tempat ujung tulang yang satu menggeser ujung tulang lainnya.

Sendi putar.

Sendi putar adalah sendi tulang yang dapat memutari tulang sambungannya.

Sendi pelana.

Sendi pelana adalah sendi yang dapat digerakkan kedua arah, misalnya gerakan kekiri dan kekanan.

39. petunjuk cara membuat api unggun dalam bahasa inggris (tugas bhs kelas8 semester 2 bab 5 task 6 )


Fire Ring Technique (Ring of Fire)
What needs to be done before making a fire with a fire loop technique, you have to make a hole first. Diameter of the hole can you adjust as needed, how big the fire you want to make. More recommended not too big, if you just few people just make a diameter of 50-60 cm. The depth of the hole you can make about 10-15 cm.

bonfire
Holes for campfire. Photo source

If the hole is ready, you can start looking for firewood or twigs as the base for making fires and then burning them in the already created holes. Try to make fire away from the trees to avoid the risk of forest fires. You also have to keep the fire not too big.

bonfire

If you feel enough with the fire you make, immediately turn off the fire by watering until completely extinguished. After that close the hole with the former fire ex-making holes. That way the fire will not come back to life, and the condition of your camping area will also come back clean as before.
