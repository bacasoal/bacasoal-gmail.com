21. tolong jawab plisa butuh buat pra un


10 - 4 = 6 cm

AE = √10²-6²
= √100-36
= √64
= 8 cm

L trapesium:
= 1/2 × (jumlah sisi sejajar) × tinggi
= 1/2 × (10 + 4) × 8
= 1/2 × 14 × 8
= 56 cm²

L segitiga = 1/2 × a × t
= 1/2 × 10 × 10
= 50 cm²

L total = 50 + 56
= 106 cm² (C)
