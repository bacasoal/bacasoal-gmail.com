8. Rewrite the sentences in passive voice elementary school students study english


Answer: 

Passive form is the sentence form used for making statement focusing on the object than the doer. The pattern is O + auxiliary verbs + V3 + by + S 
Such pattern applies for all tenses with slight alteration on the auxiliary verb depending on the tenses and the context. 
In the exercise above, the sentence is absolutely in present simple tense. So, the auxiliary verbs used is 'is' and 'are'. The passive form of that sentence is 
'English is studied by elementary school students.'

Mapel: English
Kategori: passive form

17. Andi has learned English _______ he was in the elementary school.A. for ​


Since

↪ Andi has learned English (Since) he was in the elementary school.

PEMBAHASAN

Present Perfect Tense

↪Adalah suatu jenis kalimat yang membicarakan tentang hubungan antara aksi atau kegiatan yang terjadi pada masa lalu dengan masa kini. Kalimat pada present perfect juga kadang menggunakan adverb pada kalimatnya.

➡Rumus

(+) Subject + has/have + verb 3 + object

(-) Subject + has/have + not + verb 3 + object

(?) Has/have + subject + verb 3 + object + ?

➡KeteranganHas digunakan untuk subjek singular (he, she, it)Have digunakan untuk subjek plural (I, you, we, they)

➡Contoh kalimat

(+) Sifa has eaten this food since she was in middle school

(-) Sifa has not eaten this food since she was in middle school

(?) Has Sifa eaten this food since she was in middle school?

➡Adverbs yang sering digunakan untuk jenis kalimat ini:SinceYetNeverEverEtc

➡Penjelasan:

Apabila membicarakan pengalaman seseorang pada present perfect tense biasanya menggunakan adverb "since".
